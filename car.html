Hit the Streets!

Rent a car or motorcycle and travel between communities to get a firsthand look at Kiribati's way of life and culture. Hit up mom and pop stores scattered along the road, or check out shopping complexes in Tabwakea, London, and Banana.

The main road was paved with asphault in the 1950's by allied forces while they conducted nuclear bomb testing around the area. All other roads are dirt paths.
