Location

Our hotel backs right up to the lagoon. Allowing you to enjoy the soft, white sand beach with plenty of shaded lounging areas. Plenty of palm trees and privacy add to the serenity of this pristine place.

Lagoon View Resort is located on the edge of the village of Tabwakea. Take a right at the main road (the only road) as you leave the airport. A 15 minute drive will bring you to Lagoon View Resort where a large, white sailboat welcomes you, letting you know your vacation has begun!
