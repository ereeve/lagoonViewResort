About

My name is Timei (pronounced sea-may) and I am the proud owner of Lagoon View Resort.

This hotel comes from humble beginnings, starting in 2009. As more and more people have enjoyed this hotel, I have been able to grow to accomodate more guests and make it a more enjoyable experience for everyone.

I recieve great joy in giving guests an unforgetable experience and showing off my beautiful island home.

Miscellanious information

The Australian dollar is the recognized currency in Kiribati. Plan on bringing cash for anything you may need as there are no credit card machines. There is an ATM owned by ANZ at the government building in London.

Kiribati is the official language of the island, however, the Lagoon View Resort family all speak English.
